Commonly asked questions.

— Updates & Compatibility

HCSS will always be updated to the last version of CSS and it's fully compatible with most common browsers. (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, etc.)

— Templates market & Pricing

There's no HCSS templates market yet. But if I decide to create one in the future, only big and professionnal templates will be selled for about 5$ to 10$.

— License
